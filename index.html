Progressive graphic capability — four maturity levels

Going for modern SVGs

This page illustrates a four-step progression: a legacy bitmap (PNG), a pure SVG, an SVG with hover affordances, and an SVG with hover plus clickable links.

Stage 1Baseline

Legacy bitmap (PNG)

Good for quick sharing, but limited for localisation, accessibility, responsiveness, and interactive guidance.

StaticPixel-basedLimited scaling
Stage 2Vector

Pure SVG (no interaction)

Crisp at any zoom level. Styling can be aligned with a brand palette. Still “just a picture”, but a much better one.

ScalableEditableLightweight
Stage 3Interactive

SVG with hover effects

Adds discoverability: tooltips, emphasis on hover, and a “guided reading” experience — without leaving the graphic.

Hover affordancesTooltipsExplorable
Stage 4Clickable

SVG with hover effects and links

Turns the graphic into a navigation surface: hover affordances plus link targets to specs, docs, repos, or sections.

ClickableSelf-containedGreat for GitHub Pages
